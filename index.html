Enhance your Mathematical Skills

ALGEBRA

Algebra uses symbols and letters to represent numbers and relationships, enabling the formulation and solving of equations, expressions, and functions.

CALCULUS

Calculus studies how things change through rates of change (differentiation) and accumulation (integration), and is used in physics, engineering, and science.

TRIGONOMETRY

Trigonometry studies the relationships between the angles and sides of triangles and uses functions like sine, cosine, and tangent to solve angle and distance problems.

Learn the Concepts of Chemistry

GENERAL CHEMISTRY

General chemistry is the study of matter, its structure, properties, and reactions, covering atoms, bonding, stoichiometry, states of matter, and chemical interactions.

THERMOCHEMISTRY

Every chemical reaction follows the laws of conservation of mass and energy, with accompanying energy changes.

CHEMICAL KINETICS

Chemical kinetics studies the speed of chemical reactions and the factors—like concentration, temperature, pressure, and catalysts—that affect them, providing insight into how reactions occur.
